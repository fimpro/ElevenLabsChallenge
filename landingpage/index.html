The Problem

Guided Tours

  • • Follow fixed schedules and routes
  • • High costs ($30-50/person)
  • • Limited availability and flexibility
  • • No spontaneous exploring

Exploring Alone

  • • Miss historical context and stories
  • • No local insights or hidden gems
  • • Hours picking spots to go to
  • • Planning route can be problematic, especially if you are indecisive

Both traditional guided tours and solo (no-guide) exploration have significant drawbacks. As student travelers we needed a solution that combines the knowledge and insights of a tour guide with the freedom and flexibility of exploring on their own terms and schedules.
